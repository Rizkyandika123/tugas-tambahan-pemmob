Microsoft Technology Associate

Microsoft Technology Associate (MTA) training and certification delivers essential technology skills and an industry-recognized certification to those just starting a career path in information Technology (IT), or those looking to enhance their understanding of IT fundamentals. MTA skills development instills practical knowledge on how to apply Microsoft technologies to solve today’s business challenges, and is the gateway to the advanced Microsoft Certifications that distinguish the industry’s best IT professionals and software developers.

The gateway to a rewarding IT career

MTA Certification is the gateway to advanced Microsoft Certifications. MTA provides that essential“first step”, and the baseline understanding you need to successfully pursue MCSA, MCSE and MCSD credentials. To learn more about the family of Microsoft Certifications, visit www.microsoft.com/learning/certification .

A fast, affordable way to get ahead. Microsoft Technology Associate Certification Exam

Microsoft Technology Associate (MTA) is a recommended entry point into IT certification and job preparation. Pass just one exam and you’ll earn a certification, taking your first step toward a career in technology. If you are just starting your IT career path or are looking to enhance your understanding of IT fundamentals, MTA will validate your core knowledge. MTA is an optional industry-recognized certification for those pursuing a career path in IT infrastructure, database design, or software development using Microsoft technologies.
